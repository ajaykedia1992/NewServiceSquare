Is our staff competent and trustworthy?

Our staff consists of hand picked workers with a minimum experience of 2 years. We give complete assurance regarding your satisfaction and security. A complete and comprehensive profile about each employee is available to every customer. Customer can select the worker they are comfortable with based on their feedback, comments,ratings etc.

What happens if the staff causes unintended damage to customer's property?

Our staff is well trained and strive to solve every problem that vexes the customer. However, under some unfortunate circumstances such as damage to customer's property, ServiceSquare shall take complete responsibility and the customer will be insured upto Rs. 20000 for any form of damage cause. Inconvienience under such circumstances are regretted.

What are the rating and review mechanisms in our corporation?

Ratings are based on the comments and review points submitted by the worker's previous clients and customer. Ratings are calculated based on weighted average(by year) of all the consolidated reviews and ratings for that particular worker. To summarize, the rating mechanisms are reliable and accurate to ensure that our customers are offered the best services our company has to offer.

What are the arrival time policies of the workers?

The company strives to ensure that all the booked workers arrive on time but sometimes, due to some unprecedented circumstances, a delay of maximum 30 minutes needs to be tolerated by our customer. Patience on your part will be appreciated. If there is any delay beyond 30 minutes, the customer will receive an appropriate discount for our services.

What are the means of payment for the various services?

The payment can be carried out in advance through various convenient methods such ase credit card, debit card, net banking etc. Moreover, we support 'cash after service' method as well where the customer needs to pay the wages to the allocated worker. Customer satisfaction is our primary objective.
